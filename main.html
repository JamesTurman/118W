IAMS

IAMS is an acronym for I Am Sleepy.

IAMS was created by Sir Fluffy McFluffster, who developed highly efficient methods to cook cat food.

McFluffster drew his inspiration from being lazy and always wanting to sleep, so was inspired to find a more efficient way to make good food.
